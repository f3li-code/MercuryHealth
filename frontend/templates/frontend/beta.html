About

At Mercury Health, we are committed to developing technology to help adults age in place safely, independently, and comfortably without compromising on the privacy of your loved one. Our Mercury Home device consists of a hands-free, small, and unintrusive sensor designed to detect emergency situations and instantly alert emergency contacts.

How it Works

INSTALL
Our team will install Mercury Home in a desired room.

CAPTURE + ANALYZE
Encrypted sensor data captured by the device are anonymously analyzed and emergency situation information is securely stored as client history.

ALERT
Emergency contacts/services are notified when a fall or other occurrence is detected.
