About

When you join our beta test, we will:

1) Talk to you over the phone about the details of our beta program and ensure we're a good fit for your home need.

2) Walk you through setting up an account in our online portal, where you can view health related analytics based on detections captured.

3) Install our Mercury Home device in a room you choose. We're very cautious about being germ-free, where client health and safety is our first priority! And, if you'd prefer, we can walk you through how to install it yourself over the phone.

4) Apply our detection and alerting subscription services that you're interested in for free (fall detection, lack of movement detection, sitting out of bed detection, sleep monitoring, or wandering detection) while you're enrolled in the beta program!

How Our Service Works

INSTALL
Our team will install Mercury Home in a desired room.

CAPTURE
Encrypted sensor data captured by the device are anonymously analyzed and emergency situation information is securely stored as client history.

ALERT
Emergency contacts/services are notified when a fall or other occurrence is detected.

ANALYZE
View client activity history in our user portal to spot trends in health data.
