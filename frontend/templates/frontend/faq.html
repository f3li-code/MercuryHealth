How safe is my data?

All the data captured by our device is encrypted and anonymized. This process removes any personally identifiable information from the data to ensure complete anonymity. Any data stored in our HIPAA compliant cloud service is available for you to view in your client portal.

What other possible emergencies could Mercury Home detect?

Mercury Home can detect falls, lack of movement, irregular patterns of activity, and any other actions based on your specific needs. Please contact us if you would like a personalized emergency detected.
