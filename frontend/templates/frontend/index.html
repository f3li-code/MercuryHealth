Our System

Keep your loved ones safe.

Mercury Home consists of a small, unintrusive sensor that can detect emergency situations. Emergency contacts or services will be contacted instantly if anything is wrong.

Encourage independence.

Unlike current technologies which use wearable technology (like the Apple Watch™ and Life Alert) to invoke emergency responses, Mercury Home easily installs in any desired room and senses entirely from a distance.

Personalize care.

Our device analyzes sensor data, and anything important we notice can be viewed via our online user portal.

How it Works

INSTALL
Our team will install Mercury Home in a desired room.

CAPTURE + ANALYZE
Encrypted sensor data captured by the device are anonymously analyzed and emergency situation information is securely stored as client history.

ALERT
Emergency contacts/services are notified when a fall or other occurrence is detected.
