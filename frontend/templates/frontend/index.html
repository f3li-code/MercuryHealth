Our System

We aim to bring esoteric machine learning algorithms to healthcare and keep older adults safe, independent, and comfortable. While we focus a lot on the technology, our first priority is to ensure patient health, and we use all means necessary to do so.

Keep your loved ones safe.

Our Mercury Device consists of a small, unintrusive camera that can detect elderly falls and other human actions in real time. Emergency contacts or services will be contacted instantly if anything is wrong.

Encourage independence.

Unlike current technologies which use wearable technology (like the Apple Watch™ and Life Alert) to analyze a person’s actions, our Mercury Device easily installs in any desired room and monitors entirely from a distance.

Personalize care.

Video captured is sent to our servers, which anonymously analyze the data to look for patterns in a person's movement. Anything important we notice is saved for you to view via our online patient portal.

Our Device

Wifi Streaming
Wifi is used to stream video data captured from your home to our secure servers.

Deep Learning Visual Recognition Models
The servers apply custom, proprietary Deep Learning Visual Recognition Models to analyze video data received. These algorithms look for specific person movements, similar to a person monitoring a surveillance camera.

Incident Detection
If our algorithms detect an incident, servers instantly text all registered emergency contacts and ensure help is on the way.

Activity Logging
Meanwhile your personal online profile is updated with the incident, so we can track long term patterns and help prevent future occurrences.

How it Works

INSTALL
Our team will install the Mercury Device in a desired room.

REGISTER
Create a login and add emergency contact details to receive text notifications when events are detected.

CAPTURE + IDENTIFY
Videos captured by the device are encrypted and anonymized, sent to our cloud to analyze for specific patterns in movement.

ALERT
Emergency contacts/services are notified when a fall or other occurrence is detected.
